Char Dham Yatra

Char Dham of Uttarakhand is one of the most important Hindu Pilgrimages in India where thousands of people visit every year to seek spirituality within. Experience this through this extensive package from Haridwar and get close to the enchanting prayers of Hinduism.

Short Itinerary

  • 📅 Day 1: Haridwar to Barkot
  • 📅 Day 2: Barkot – Janki Chatti – Yamunotri – Back to Barkot
  • 📅 Day 3: Barkot to Uttarkashi
  • 📅 Day 4: Uttarkashi – Gangotri – Back to Uttarkashi
  • 📅 Day 5: Uttarkashi to Guptkashi
  • 📅 Day 6: Guptkashi to Kedarnath
  • 📅 Day 7: Kedarnath to Guptkashi
  • 📅 Day 8: Guptkashi to Badrinath
  • 📅 Day 9: Badrinath to Haridwar

Char Dham Tour - Itinerary

Day 1: Haridwar to Barkot (220 km / 7-8 hrs)

  • 🚗 Depart from Haridwar in the morning.
  • 🌊 En route, visit Mussoorie and Kempty Falls (optional).
  • 🏨 Arrive in Barkot by evening and check in at the hotel.
  • 🛌 Rest and prepare for Yamunotri’s trek the next day.
  • 🌙 Overnight stay in Barkot.

Day 2: Barkot – Jankichatti – Yamunotri – Barkot (36 km drive & 6 km trek one way)

  • 🚗 Early morning drive to Jankichatti (trek starting point).
  • 🥾 Trek (or hire a pony/palki) to Yamunotri Temple.
  • ♨️ Visit Surya Kund and Divya Shila, offer prayers at the temple.
  • 🚶‍♂️ Return trek to Jankichatti and drive back to Barkot.
  • 🌙 Overnight stay in Barkot.

Day 3: Barkot to Uttarkashi (100 km / 4-5 hrs)

  • 🚗 Depart for Uttarkashi, a spiritual town.
  • 🛕 Visit Kashi Vishwanath Temple and Shakti Temple.
  • 🚶‍♂️ Evening exploration or rest.
  • 🌙 Overnight stay in Uttarkashi.

Day 4: Uttarkashi – Gangotri – Uttarkashi (100 km one way / 3-4 hrs)

  • 🚗 Early morning drive along the Bhagirathi River to Gangotri.
  • 🛕 Visit Gangotri Temple and take a holy dip.
  • 🌳 Explore nearby attractions like Pandav Gufa (optional).
  • 🚗 Return to Uttarkashi in the evening.
  • 🌙 Overnight stay in Uttarkashi.

Day 5: Uttarkashi to Guptkashi (220 km / 8-9 hrs)

  • 🚗 Early departure for Guptkashi via Tehri Dam and Rudraprayag.
  • 🛕 Visit Ardh Narishwar Temple in Guptkashi.
  • 🛌 Rest and prepare for the Kedarnath trek.
  • 🌙 Overnight stay in Guptkashi.

Day 6: Guptkashi to Kedarnath (30 km drive & 18 km trek/heli option available)

  • 🚗 Drive to Gaurikund (trek starting point).
  • 🥾 Trek to Kedarnath (or use a pony, palki, or helicopter).
  • 🛕 Visit Kedarnath Temple and attend the evening aarti.
  • 🌙 Overnight stay in Kedarnath.

Day 7: Kedarnath to Guptkashi (18 km trek & 30 km drive)

  • 🌄 Early morning darshan at Kedarnath Temple.
  • 🚶‍♂️ Trek back to Gaurikund and drive to Guptkashi.
  • 🛌 Rest and recover after the trek.
  • 🌙 Overnight stay in Guptkashi.

Day 8: Guptkashi to Badrinath (190 km / 7-8 hrs)

  • 🚗 Drive to Badrinath via Joshimath and Vishnuprayag.
  • 🛕 Visit Badrinath Temple for evening aarti.
  • ♨️ Explore Tapt Kund (hot springs) and Mana Village.
  • 🌙 Overnight stay in Badrinath.

Day 9: Badrinath to Haridwar (310 km / 9-10 hrs)

  • 🌄 Early morning darshan at Badrinath Temple.
  • 🚗 Drive back to Haridwar, visiting sacred confluences en route.
  • 🙏 Arrive in Haridwar by evening—Yatra ends with divine blessings!
