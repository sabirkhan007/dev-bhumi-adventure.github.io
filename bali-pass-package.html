Bali Pass Trek - Itinerary

Day 1: Dehradun to Sankri (6,400ft)

Your first day will start at Dehradun Railway Station. You will drive for just about 10 hours to arrive at Sankri. Sankri town lies in the scope of Govind Wildlife Sanctuary, Uttarkashi. In transit from Dehradun to Sankri, you will cross different wonderful landscape of nature. You will partake in this excursion. You will go over Kempty Waterfalls, Nainbagh, and some more. The town is encircled by a few pinnacles like Swargrohini and some more.

In the wake of arriving at Sankri, you will begin feeling the genuine frenzy and energy for the impending journey. In the event that you reach early, you might investigate the town. You will see a few houses comprised of mud, dhabas that serve conventional food, local people in customary outfits, and considerably more. You will remain in the visitor house for the time being.

Day 2: Sankri to Seema via Taluka (6,400ft to 8,500ft via 6,700ft)

In the wake of eating at the visitor house, you will continue to Taluka through drive. Then, at that point in the wake of arriving at Taluka, your trip will begin. You will begin your journey alongside the Supin stream. You will see numerous normal things coming. Water streams are an incredible wellspring of water coming. As you push ahead you will pass different extensions of wood just as concrete. Then, at that point you will arrive at an open ground after some distance.

After some time, you will reach Ghangad Village where you can see many locals who will treat you with a sweet gesture. After crossing the beautiful path full of dhabas and water streams, you will reach Osla village, a famous tourist spot. From here it will take around an hour to reach Seema. You will see various flora & fauna. At Seema, you will stay in tents and will rest overnight.

Day 3: Seema to Rainbasera (8,500ft to 10,200ft)

Your third day will be a journey to Rainbasera. This journey will likewise oblige the Supin River. From the start, you will spot Debshu Bugyal. Subsequent to strolling ahead through the precarious path you will see an incredible perspective on Oslo town behind.

Debshu Bugyal is huge to such an extent that you can take a rest over. As you will see around yourself, you will actually want to see tops like Kala Nag. The way forward in this knoll is dangerous in nature so it is fitting to stroll with most extreme consideration. Do pay attention to your aide's guidelines and follow them appropriately. There will be a lofty ascension from there on. Before long, you will arrive at Rainbasera, your camping area for the afternoon. Set up your shelters and appreciate quality time with companions and stay in tents for the time being.

Day 4: Rainbasera to Ruinsara Tal

You will partake in this current day's trip as it is loaded with shocks. Also, the objective for the day is Ruinsara Tal which is astonishing in itself. In the wake of strolling some distance, you will see ice sheets and a few water streams. This current day's campground, i.e Ruinsara Tal will make you insane by its most extreme excellence in and around itself. The total is situated among glades and looks charming. Day 5: Ruinsara Tal to Odari (11,800ft to 13,100ft.)

This day will be totally committed to investigate the regions as on this day there will be a short trek,i.e of 4 km. You will travel by means of woods and will likewise cross waterway. You will discover steep way during the journey and afterward will arrive at glade. While being at knoll, you will see such countless higher pinnacles. After some time, you will arrive at Odari. Odari implies Cave, henceforth you will see a stone cavern here. You will get an opportunity to investigate the spot totally. Guide might give you some instructional course that will help you for additional journeys. Stay in Odari in tents.

Day 6: Odari to Bali col camp (13,100ft to 15,100ft)

This day you will find an inclined path with a steep climb. This day's trail is difficult. During this trek you must take care a little more as the rocks in this area are loose in nature. Do fill your water bottles time to time. You will climb a great height and hence will experience a new adventure. Do take proper precautions while trekking here. You will stay in tents overnight and the place becomes cold in night so make sure to carry warm clothes.

Day 7: Bali col camp to Lower Dhamni via Bali Pass

It will be the best day of your Bali Pass Trek. You will reach Lower Dhami on this day via Bali Pass. You must fulfill yourself with energy so as to remain active throughout the trek. On this trek, you will see so much snow.

After walking for about 2-3 hours you will find yourself at the top of the pass. It will be the best feeling while standing at the top of the pass. You will see a crystal clear view of Swargarohini, Banderpooch and several other garhwal peaks. There lies Yamunotri Valley on the right side. This place will provide you several sight views which are worth clicking pictures. Now you will descend which is much more exciting to experience. But you must be careful while descending. As the place is completely covered with snow, you need to be very careful. Just follow your guide and stay alert.

Then after taking a rest for sometime, you will again start your trek to Lower Dhami. This trek will be straight & simple in nature. The path of the trek goes via forests. And finally you will reach your campsite where you will stay in tents for the last day of your Bali Pass Trek.

Day 8: Lower Dhamni to Jaan ki Chatti and Drive to Dehradun

This is the last day of your trip. You will trip to Jaan ki Chatti which will be a combination of drops. Then, at that point in the wake of arriving at the campground, you will leave for Dehradun.
