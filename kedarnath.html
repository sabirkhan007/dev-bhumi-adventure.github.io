Kedarnath Yatra

Trek to a place where divinity murmurs in the air! Kedarnath, a place known as an abode of Lord Shiva captivates you with the glory it holds. It is a famous Hindu pilgrimage destination where thousands of devotees from all over the world come in pursuit to attain ‘Moksha’.

Short Itinerary

  • Day 1: Haridwar - Phata
  • Day 2: Phata - Gaurikund - Kedarnath
  • Day 3: Kedarnath - Phata
  • Day 4: Phata - Haridwar

Kedarnath Yatra - Itinerary

Day 1: Haridwar - Phata

You will be picked from Haridwar in the morning and will be taken to Phata. One can also witness the beauty of the yoga capital of India. The route will be covering Byasi, Devprayag, Srinagar, Rudraprayag, and Agastmuni hill stations on the way. Have your lunch in the midway and you will reach the destination in the evening. Check-in into the hotel and enjoy the evening. Overnight stay at Phata/Guptkashi.

Day 2: Phata - Gaurikund - Kedarnath

Wake up with the mesmerizing view of mountains all across in Phata. Before embarking towards Gaurikund, have a delicious breakfast in the morning. Gauri Kund is a place where Lord Shiva finally admitted His love for Lord Gauri as well as it is a characteristic pool with warm water known for relieving skin maladies. Start the trek to Kedarnath from Gauri Kund. You can take ponies or palanquins for the journey at your own cost. Trek through the beautiful valley and river stream flowing in between both of them. Have a lunch stop in between and continue the journey, you will get to see the Kedarnath Temple, situated right at the center of the valley with mountains on every side looking like they are providing safety to the temples. After offering prayers at Kedarnath, have dinner & take a rest. Overnight stay and dinner will be at Kedarnath.

Day 3: Kedarnath - Phata

In the morning, all the participants can visit the Kedarnath temple. Explore the views of the Himalayas, Bhimshila, and Mandakini river. Do not forget to click memories for life with your mates. After visiting the temple, We will trek back towards Sonprayag. Night stay and dinner will be at Phata/Guptkashi.
