Kedarnath Yatra

Trek to a place where divinity murmurs in the air! Kedarnath, a place known as an abode of Lord Shiva captivates you with the glory it holds. It is a famous Hindu pilgrimage destination where thousands of devotees from all over the world come in pursuit to attain ‘Moksha’.

Short Itinerary

  • Day 1: Haridwar to Guptkashi
  • Day 2: Guptkashi to kedarnath
  • Day 3: Kedarnath to Guptkashi
  • Day 4: Guptkashi to haridwar

Kedarnath Yatra - Itinerary

Day 1: Haridwar to Guptkashi (220 km | 8-9 hours)

Morning: Depart from Haridwar early in the morning after a hearty breakfast. Enjoy a scenic drive through the winding roads of Uttarakhand, passing through Rishikesh, Devprayag (the confluence of the Alaknanda and Bhagirathi rivers), and Rudraprayag.

Afternoon: Halt for lunch en route and continue the journey through picturesque landscapes.

Dinner & Stay: Enjoy a hot dinner and overnight stay at the hotel in Guptkashi.

Day 2: Guptkashi to Kedarnath (30 km drive + 16 km trek)

Early Morning: Have breakfast and proceed towards Gaurikund, the base point for the Kedarnath trek.

Trekking Options: Trek on foot (16 km) Hire a pony or palanquin (at additional cost) Take a helicopter (pre-booking required)

En Route: Witness breathtaking views of snow-capped peaks, lush green valleys, and the Mandakini River flowing alongside.

Evening: Attend the Kedarnath Aarti, a spiritually uplifting experience at the Kedarnath Temple, one of the twelve Jyotirlingas of Lord Shiva.

Dinner & Stay: Overnight stay at Kedarnath.

Day 3: Kedarnath to Guptkashi (16 km trek + 30 km drive)

Early Morning: Wake up early for the Abhishek Puja at Kedarnath Temple (optional).

Morning: Begin the descent to Gaurikund after breakfast.

Afternoon: Reach Gaurikund and drive back to Guptkashi. Check into the hotel and relax.

Evening: Explore Guptkashi at leisure or rest after the tiring trek.

Dinner & Stay: Overnight stay at the hotel in Guptkashi.

Day 4: Guptkashi to Haridwar (220 km | 8-9 hours)

Morning: Have breakfast and begin the return journey to Haridwar .

Afternoon: Stop at Rudraprayag and Devprayag for sightseeing and lunch.

Evening: Arrive in Haridwar by late evening. Tour concludes with divine memories.
