Kedarkantha Trek - Itinerary

Day 1: Dehradun -Sankri/ Mandal [drive:7-8 hrs Distance:188kms/3hrs]

The thrilling trek towards Kedarkantha begins from Dehradun city. Our representative meets you at the Dehradun Railway Station at 7:00 a.m. From here, drive alongside the scenic views of river Yamuna and hill towns like Purola and Mori to reach Sankri/Mandal at around 4:00 p.m.. On arrival at Sankri/Mandal check into you cozyguest house/Campsite. The day is yours to explore the culture and local cuisines this place has to offer.Get back to your stay and have a good sleep as the day you waited for starts tomorrow.

Day 2: Sankri -Juda [Trek: -4-5hrs]

Post breakfast, trek 4 kms to the camping base - Juda Ka Talab and reach there in about 5 hours. The trekking trail crosses streams and dense oak forests, rising through which you reach the pictorial spot of Jadu Ka Talab. Halt for a day and enjoy photography andb camping at this beautiful scenery.

Day 3: Juda - Kedarkantha Base /Shepherd' Hurts[ Trek:3-4hrs]

Post breakfast, ascend towards Kedarkantha base which will take about 3 to 4 hours. Pass through the dense pine and oak forests and shepherdsâ€™ huts upon open meadows.Take in the fascinating views of an arc-shaped range of snow-capped mountains comprising of Bandarpoonch, Swargarohini, Kala Nag and Ranglana - you will reach the Kedarkantha Base by the day. After reaching the base, heck into the camps and enjoy the scenery of snow-capped mountains, clear skies as well as a mesmerizing sunset over the mighty Himalayas.

Day 4: Kedarkantha Base - Kedarkantha Summit and descend -Juda [Trek:7]

The long awaited day begins as you make your final climb towards the Kedarkantha summit.The day starts early with the Sunrise from the Kedarkantha meadows and a filling breakfast.You trek through the forest till the end, as the Kedarkantha peak appears closer and the vegetation starts to disappear. After some more trek, you reach the summit. At an elevation of 12,500, stand tall and shout out aloud â€œWe did itâ€.Rest for sometime, and start the descend back towards Juda, and halt for the day there to celebrate.

Day 5: Juda ka Talab -Sankri- to Dehradun[Trek: 4km]

Post breakfast, start the descend towards sankri On the way, mesmerize the final glimpses of Har Ki Dun valley and capture the memories.Reach Sankri at around 12:00 p.m. to 1:00 pm .Depart for Dehradun. The distance approximatelytakes about 8 hours to reach. Scenic ambience and lovely surroundings mark the roads back to Dehradun city
