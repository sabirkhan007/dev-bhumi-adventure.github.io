Rupin and Supin Trek - Itinerary

Day 1: Dehradun to Khanyisani Camp
  • 🚗 Total Distance: 235 km – 9 to 10 hours journey
  • ⛰️ Altitude: Khanyisani Camp – 1878 meters
  • 🏨 Night Stay: Campsite – twin share basis
  • 🕕 Pickup: Dehradun Railway Station between 6:00 AM to 7:00 AM
  • 🌄 Scenic drive through Mussoorie, Kempty Falls, Yamuna River, Nainbagh, Purola, and Jarmola
  • ☕ Tea/snacks on arrival at Khanyisani campsite
Day 2: Khanyisani Camp to Sarutal Camp
  • 🥾 Trek Distance: 8 km – 4 to 5 hours
  • ⛰️ Altitude: Sarutal Camp – 2000 meters
  • 🏕️ Night Stay: Campsite – twin share basis
  • 🍏 Walk past apple orchards and traditional villages like Pujali and Bitri
  • 🌲 Trek through dense pine forest with views of Himachal and Changsheel Valley
Day 3: Sarutal Camp to Badang Camp
  • 🥾 Trek Distance: 8 km – 5 to 6 hours
  • ⛰️ Altitude: Badang Camp – 2600 meters
  • 🏕️ Night Stay: Campsite – twin share basis
  • 🏞️ View of Swargarohini peaks and stunning Himalayan ridgelines
  • 💧 Cross streams and walk through thick forests to wide green meadow
Day 4: Badang Camp to Pachi Thach via Vijay Top
  • 🥾 Trek Distance: 12 km – 5 to 6 hours
  • ⛰️ Altitude: Vijay Top – 3800 meters
  • 🏕️ Night Stay: Campsite – twin share basis
  • 🌄 Panoramic views of Vishkhopri, Obra Valley, and Khimloga Pass
  • 🌼 Meadows filled with colorful wildflowers, thick forests during descent
Day 5: Pachi Thach to Fhitari Village & Drive to Sankri
  • 🥾 Trek Distance: 7 km – 4 to 5 hours
  • 🚗 Drive Distance: 36 km
  • ⛰️ Altitude: Sankri – 1920 meters
  • 🏨 Night Stay: nature view himalaya resort – twin share basis
  • 🌾 Pass farms and villages like Fhitari and Dhara, and Someshwar temple near Supin River
  • 🧑‍🌾 Interact with locals in Jakhol village, the gateway to Supin Valley
Day 6: Drive from Sankri to Dehradun
  • 🚗 Total Distance: 210 km – 8 to 9 hours journey
  • 🍳 Final Breakfast: With Himalayan Hikers team
  • 📸 Memories: Certificate distribution and group photos
  • 👋 Goodbye: Departure for Dehradun and onward journey
