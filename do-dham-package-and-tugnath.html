Do Dham & Tungnath Trek - Itinerary

Day 1: Day 1: Haridwar to Guptkashi (220 km | 8-9 hours)

Morning: Depart from Haridwar early after breakfast. En Route: Visit Devprayag (confluence of Alaknanda and Bhagirathi rivers) and Rudraprayag. Afternoon: Lunch break during the journey. Evening: Arrive in Guptkashi, check into the hotel, and relax. Dinner & Stay: Overnight stay at the hotel in Guptkashi.

Day 2: Guptkashi to Kedarnath (30 km drive + 16 km trek)

Early Morning: Depart for Gaurikund, the base for the Kedarnath trek. Trekking Options: Trek on foot (16 km) Hire a pony or palanquin Helicopter service available (pre-booking required) Afternoon: Reach Kedarnath, check into your accommodation. Evening: Visit Kedarnath Temple, attend Aarti, and explore the sacred surroundings. Dinner & Stay: Overnight stay in Kedarnath.

Day 3: Kedarnath to Guptkashi (16 km trek + 30 km drive)

Early Morning: Attend Abhishek Puja at Kedarnath Temple (optional). Morning: After breakfast, trek down to Gaurikund. Afternoon: Drive back to Guptkashi and check into the hotel. Evening: Rest and relax after the trek. Dinner & Stay: Overnight stay at the hotel in Guptkashi.

Day 4: Guptkashi to Badrinath (200 km | 8-9 hours)

Morning: After breakfast, begin the journey towards Badrinath.
En Route: Stop at Joshimath, Vishnuprayag, and Pandukeshwar.
Afternoon: Reach Badrinath Temple, check into the hotel, and visit Tapt Kund (hot water spring).
Evening: Attend the Badrinath Aarti and explore Mana Village (the last village of India).
Dinner & Stay: Overnight stay at the hotel in Badrinath.

Day 5: Badrinath to Haridwar (320 km | 10-12 hours)

Early Morning: Visit Badrinath Temple for darshan.
Morning: After breakfast, start the return journey.
En Route: Stop at Rudraprayag, Devprayag, and Rishikesh for sightseeing.
Evening: Arrive in Haridwar, marking the end of the spiritual journey.
